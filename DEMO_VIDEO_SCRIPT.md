# 55-Second Demo Script

Record this from the public Reddit post, not localhost.

0-05s: Show the post and the visual clue. "SubCipher asks players to identify a Reddit community from its culture."

05-15s: Open the game. Show the clue, riddle, six-attempt board, and keyboard. "Every player gets the same daily case."

15-25s: Enter a wrong guess. Show green, yellow, and muted letter feedback, then reveal the next hint. "A miss gives you more evidence, but costs score."

25-35s: Solve the case. Show the score, streak, and spoiler-safe result text. "The answer and score are checked by the server."

35-45s: Open the leaderboard. "The daily result is shared, so the comment thread has one common puzzle to discuss."

45-55s: Show the explicit result-post action. "Players choose whether to share a spoiler-safe result to Reddit."
